Honor whom honor deserves

As students, much of our knowledge depends in a way of our learnings during school time. Professors are an essential piece in our learning process. The way they share their skills and expertise can greatly influence students lives. For those professors that made a difference in our lives is intended this project. To show them our infinite gratitude.

About us

The idea of this Website emerged in the computer science classrooms of the Florida International University. It was inspired by a brilliant professor whose teachings opened the doors of our successful careers as programmers: Norman Pestaina. For all your wisdom, advice and support, THANK YOU.
